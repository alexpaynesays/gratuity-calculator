How to Use a Gratuity Calculator in the UAE?

What is an End of Service Calculator?

An end of service calculator is a financial tool designed to help employees estimate their gratuity or end-of-service benefits when leaving a job. This calculator simplifies the process by allowing users to input their basic salary, years of service, and contract type to receive an instant estimate of their gratuity entitlement.

Factors Influencing Gratuity Calculation

Several key factors influence the amount of gratuity an employee can expect to receive:

1. Basic Salary

The basic salary is the primary component in gratuity calculations. Only the basic salary is considered, excluding allowances, bonuses, or other benefits.

2. Duration of Service

The length of service is critical for gratuity calculation. Employees must complete at least one year of continuous service to be eligible for gratuity. The gratuity amount varies based on the number of years worked:

1 to 5 years: 21 days of salary for each year of service.

5 years and above: 30 days of salary for each additional year.

3. Type of Employment Contract

The type of contract—limited or unlimited—affects gratuity calculations:

Limited Contracts:

These specify a fixed term of employment. If an employee resigns before the contract ends, they may still be entitled to gratuity based on the duration of service.

Unlimited Contracts:

Employees on unlimited contracts have more flexibility regarding termination. The gratuity calculation remains the same, but the conditions for termination may vary.
